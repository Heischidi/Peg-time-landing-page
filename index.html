About

By Winnipeggers,
for Winnipeggers.

PegTime was born out of sheer frustration. We got tired of standing outside in -30°C staring at a bus that Google Maps promised was "on time." So we built something smarter.

We're a small team of Winnipeg Transit riders who've spent too many mornings frozen at a bus stop on Portage, Pembina, or Graham waiting for buses that run late by habit, not by accident. PegTime learns those habits — by route, by hour, by weather, by day of week — and tells you exactly when you actually need to leave your front door.

No GPS drain. No guessing. No more arriving 15 minutes early to stand in a blizzard. Just a smart leave-by notification so you stay warm until the last possible second.

App Flow

How PegTime works for you

Three simple steps between you and never freezing at a bus stop again.

01

Pick your stop

Enter your Winnipeg Transit stop number or tap a route on the map. No account setup. No permissions. Just your stop.

e.g. Portage & Vaughan — Stop 10060
02

PegTime crunches the history

We analyze real historical delay data for your exact route — factoring in the day of week, time of day, Winnipeg weather, and known bottlenecks like Pembina construction or Graham Ave bunching.

94%+ prediction accuracy
03

You get the real leave-by time

Instead of the bus schedule, you get a single clear alert: "Your Route 11 is running 9 minutes late. Leave home at 5:16 PM — not 5:00." Stay warm inside until the last second.

🔔 Smart push notification

Engine Flow

A transit app that gets smarter

Traditional transit schedules rely on perfect conditions. PegTime learns from patterns, not hopeful clocks.

01

Enter Stop / Route

Simply enter your local bus stop number or select the route you rely on. No background battery-draining GPS tracking required.

02

Analyze Delay History

PegTime analyzes actual delays on this day, at this exact hour, under current Winnipeg weather (-30°C blizzard vs summer rain).

03

Get Leave-By Notification

Get a clear alert: "Your 8:12 Pembina bus is delayed by 14 minutes. Do not leave your house until 8:22." Zero freezing at the stop.
