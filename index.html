Engine Flow

A transit app that gets smarter

Traditional transit schedules rely on perfect conditions. PegTime learns from patterns, not hopeful clocks.

01

Enter Stop / Route

Simply enter your local bus stop number or select the route you rely on. No background battery-draining GPS tracking required.

02

Analyze Delay History

PegTime analyzes actual delays on this day, at this exact hour, under current Winnipeg weather (-30°C blizzard vs summer rain).

03

Get Leave-By Notification

Get a clear alert: "Your 8:12 Pembina bus is delayed by 14 minutes. Do not leave your house until 8:22." Zero freezing at the stop.
